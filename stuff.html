Outer Banks Activity & Dining Picks
Aug 23–30
Base: Scarborough Lane, Duck · Beach/Pool daily · Willing to drive up to ~2 hrs

Activities (ranked)

#1

Wild Horse Tour (Carova/Corolla)

Guided 4×4 beach tour to see the famous wild horses roaming the dunes. Memorable and unique to OBX.

Drive ~40 min Time 2–3 hrs Cost ~$50–60 adult / ~$30–40 child
#2

Jockey’s Ridge State Park

Tallest sand dunes on the East Coast. Great for climbing, kite flying, and hang‑gliding lessons.

Drive ~50 min Time 1–2 hrs Cost Free (lessons extra)
#3

Duck Boardwalk & Town Park

Soundside boardwalk with marsh views, shops, and sunsets. Easy stroll before or after dinner.

Drive ~5 min Time 30–90 min Cost Free
#4

Kayak or SUP on Currituck Sound

Calm, protected waters—perfect for first‑timers and kids. Nor’Banks and other Duck outfitters offer rentals.

Drive 5–10 min Time 1–3 hrs Cost ~$30–40 rental
#5

Currituck Beach Lighthouse (Corolla)

Climb the brick lighthouse for sweeping Outer Banks views; pair with Whalehead grounds nearby.

Drive ~35 min Time 30–60 min Cost ~$12 pp
#6

Wright Brothers National Memorial

Walk the first‑flight grounds and exhibits where aviation history began in 1903.

Drive ~35 min Time 1–2 hrs Cost ~$10 pp
#7

NC Aquarium on Roanoke Island (Manteo)

Compact but engaging aquarium with touch tanks, sharks, and otters—easy add‑on to a Manteo day.

Drive ~1 hr 15 min Time 1–2 hrs Cost ~$15 adult / ~$13 child
#8

Roanoke Island Festival Park (Manteo)

Living‑history village and 16th‑century ship replica with hands‑on exhibits by the waterfront.

Drive ~1 hr 20 min Time 2–3 hrs Cost ~$12 adult / ~$8 child
#9

Elizabethan Gardens (Manteo)

Shaded botanical garden with statuary and sound breezes—lovely quiet interlude.

Drive ~1 hr 20 min Time 1–1.5 hrs Cost ~$12 adult / ~$8 child
#10

Dolphin Watching Cruise (Corolla or Manteo)

Relaxing boat ride to spot wild dolphins—often very successful, especially in warmer months.

Drive 40 min (Corolla) / 1 hr 20 min (Manteo) Time ~2 hrs Cost ~$30–40 adult / ~$20–25 child
#11

Jennette’s Pier (Nags Head)

Stroll above the surf or drop a line from one of the East Coast’s premier educational piers.

Drive ~55 min Time 30–60 min Cost ~$2 walk / ~$10 fish
#12

Whalehead Club House & Grounds (Corolla)

Restored 1920s Art Nouveau mansion with beautiful soundside lawns—great photo spot.

Drive ~35 min Time 1–2 hrs Cost ~$10–15 house tour (grounds free)
#13

Mini Golf (Kitty Hawk / KDH)

Pirate and jungle themed courses—easy, lighthearted outing after beach time.

Drive ~35–40 min Time ~1 hr Cost ~$10–12 pp
#14

Ocracoke Island Day Trip (via Hatteras Ferry)

Charming village, lighthouse, and uncrowded beaches. Full‑day adventure with scenic ferry ride.

Drive ~2 hrs to ferry + ~1 hr ferry Time 8–10 hrs Cost Ferry ~$15–20/car (passengers free)
#15

Cape Hatteras Lighthouse

Iconic black‑and‑white stripes; climb for a windswept panorama when conditions allow.

Drive ~2 hrs Time 1–2 hrs Cost ~$10 pp
