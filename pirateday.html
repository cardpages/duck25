Thursday, August 28 — Pirates & Lighthouses Adventure Day!

Theme: Scenic NC‑12, lighthouse grounds, shipwreck history, pirate‑style dinner
Route: Duck → Buxton (Cape Hatteras Light) → Hatteras Village (Museum) → Bodie Island → Kill Devil Hills → Duck
Departure: 10:00 AM from rental
🗺️ Google Maps route overview

🚗 10:00 AM — Scenic drive to Hatteras

  • Roll south on NC‑12 through the Cape Hatteras National Seashore with relaxed pacing and built‑in pull‑off time for photos and breathers.
  • Photo‑op options:
    • Pea Island Refuge overlooks🔗 open in maps — marsh & sound vistas; quick boardwalk and wildlife viewing.
    • Rodanthe “Jug‑Handle” Bridge🔗 open in maps — striking S‑curve bridge over Pamlico Sound; dramatic water views.
    • Bonner Bridge Pier / Oregon Inlet🔗 open in maps — former bridge span turned pedestrian pier; boats, birds, and inlet panoramas.
    • Haulover Day Use Area (“Canadian Hole”)🔗 open in maps — famous wind/kite spot on the sound; easy in‑and‑out lot.
~2 hr drive + 30 min for photo‑ops

🗼 12:30 PM — Cape Hatteras Lighthouse (grounds)

  • Walk the keeper’s quarters area and boardwalks, snap lighthouse and ocean‑side shots, browse the small shop.
  • Climb remains closed for restoration; the grounds and facilities are open.
30–60 min activity

🍴 1:30 PM — Lunch / snack (TBD)

  • Quick bite in Buxton/Hatteras Village—aim for something fast so the museum isn’t rushed.
  • Great moment for water refill and restroom break.
~30 min activity

⚓ 2:15 PM — Graveyard of the Atlantic Museum

  • Highlights: Outer Banks shipwrecks, U‑boats, lifesaving stations, and pirate lore—kid‑friendly exhibits with engaging artifacts.
  • Free admission; a focused pass through the main galleries keeps energy up for the ride north.
45–60 min activity

🚗 3:30 PM — Drive north to Bodie Island Lighthouse

  • Head back up NC‑12 along dunes and sound—golden‑hour light starts to get nice on the way.
~1 hr 30 min drive

🗼 5:00 PM — Bodie Island Lighthouse

  • Short stop for photos, a stroll on the boardwalk to the overlook, and a breather before dinner.
~30 min activity

🍽️ 6:00 PM — Dinner at Jolly Roger (Kill Devil Hills)

  • Classic OBX spot with pirate‑y décor and hearty plates; easy parking and casual vibe for mixed ages.
  • Nice chance to warm up indoors after a breezy day on NC‑12.
~1 hr 15 min activity

🏠 7:15 PM — Return to rental

  • Short final leg back up the Beach Road/US‑158 to Duck; home by early evening.
~25 min drive
